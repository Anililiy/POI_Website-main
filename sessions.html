How the Sessions Work Together

It is preferable to attend both the online and in-person sessions, as the online webinars lay the foundation for the in-person debates. However, we aim to record the online webinars and send them out for those unable to make them. Our competitions (Session 3) are completely optional and just a bit of fun!
